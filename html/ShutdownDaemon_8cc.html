Detailed Description

Implementation of a gracefull shutdown daemon.

It will send all processes SIGTERM. Since critical daemons like MallocDaemon and TimerDaemon do not require cleanup operations and are required for further shutdown sequence, these daemons block SIGTERM and remaing running. All other processes will (by default) terminate via abort(), which will call eventually do_gracefull_exit_funs().

When all processes finished handling the SIGTERM, the kShutdown() syscall will be executed. Even when processes fail to terminate, the kShutdown() will be called after 30 seconds (when timers are supported), regardless of the state of the system.
